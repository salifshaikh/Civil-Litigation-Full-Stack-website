What is Civil Litigation?

Civil litigation refers to the legal process in which civil matters are resolved in a court of law. It involves cases where individuals or businesses seek to resolve disputes, enforce rights, or obtain compensation through the court system. Unlike criminal cases, civil litigation does not involve criminal charges but rather focuses on private disputes between parties.
